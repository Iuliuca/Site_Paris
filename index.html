On my way to Paris

It was Friday afternoon, when my parents and I arrived at Timișoara. I was very nervous, because I had never been on a plane before. Of course, I was excited, but I was also scared. What if I loose my parents in the airport, or what if we got on the wrong plane?

Soon, all my fears were gone, and I gave way to happiness and excitment. After we took off, the staff came, they gave us a bottle of water and a tiny biscuit, which was very tasty, by the way :). It took about 2h to get to Munich, and then we had to wait for about 1h 30 mins to get to the next plane, that wolud take us to Paris.

We arrived in Paris, at about 1:00 AM, and then we took an Uber, a Mercedes. The airport in Paris is HUGE. It was like a city, but for Planes! But it's not as beautiful (from the inside) as the Munich airport. The airport in Munich is very big, and VERY modern, lots and lots of food :).

The next morning, we woke up at 9:00 AM, and then we went out to buy some food, and we bought a delicious chicken sandwich. It was absolutely delicious.
